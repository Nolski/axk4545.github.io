About the City of Chicago

Chicago is the third largest city in the United States by population. It is the county seat of Cook County in the state of Illinois. It currently has a population of 2.7 million with a total of 10 approximately million in the surrounding metropolitan area. Over 52 million people visited the city in 2015 according to the mayors office. It was ranked at number 7 in A T Kearny's 2014 Global Cities Index.
